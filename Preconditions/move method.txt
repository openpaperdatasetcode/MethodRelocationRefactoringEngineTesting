    P4 & The method to be moved is not a constructor. & \\
    P5 & The method to be moved is not static. & \\
    P6 & The method to be moved must either have at least one parameter of the target class type, or its enclosing class must have at least one field of the target class type. & \\
    P7 & The method must be an instance method. & \\
    P8 & The method to be moved exists. & \\
    P9 & The body of the method to be moved is valid. & \\
